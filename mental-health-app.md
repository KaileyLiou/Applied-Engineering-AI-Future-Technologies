# Waves - Mental Health Thunkable App

This is a mental health app called Waves that was made on Thunkable as the final project for the MIT BWSI program. It allows users to journal, track their mood, and perform breathing exercises. The app also has background meditation music and a guided breathing circle.

[Live App Link](https://x.thunkable.com/projectPage/69346c83d807c84f92b3f4c3)
